What We Offer



Plants thrive in various environments worldwide.

Plants are found in deserts, forests, wetlands, and even mountains. Cacti, oaks, and lilies.

Herbs, shrubs, and trees are key plant types.

Herbs are soft-stemmed, shrubs are bushy, and trees grow tall with woody stems.

Flowering plants are the most diverse group.

They produce seeds inside flowers that attract bees. Roses, tulips, and daisies.

Non-flowering plants reproduce using tiny spores.

They lack flowers or fruits and grow in damp areas. Ferns, mosses, and liverworts

Aquatic plants live fully or partially in water.

They float, root, or submerge in ponds and lakes. Lotus, duckweed, and water lilies.

Succulents store water in their thick fleshy leaves.

They survive dry, harsh climates with minimal rainfall. Aloe vera, jade, echeveria.
